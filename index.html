Make keeping track of your water easy.

Use one of our preset options to add water. You can select preset amounts, such as 360 mL or 960 mL, which reflect everyday sizes such as a water bottle. Or, you can add your own amounts. With oueve, it's not a hassle to remember how much water you drank.

Watch your progress.

Haven't been drinking a lot of water lately? oueve allows you to record your drinking habits. You'll be able to tell if you need to drink more water more easily, as all of that information is in a single place.

Why oueve?



Stay hydrated

Did you know that your body is composed of about 60% water? To maintain this healthy balance of fluids, Oueve helps keep track of your water consumption, so you'll know if you're on track.


Help your kidneys

Your body constantly needs water to flush out waste from your body. Oueve makes sure that you drink enough water to ensure that your body is working the way it should.


Perform better

Your muscle tissue is composed of 75% water, so drinking water will boost your athletic performance!1

Mission statement

Here at oueve, we're committed to keeping you healthy. That's why we're creating oueve, an app that makes sure that you stay hydrated.
